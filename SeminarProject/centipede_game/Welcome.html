How the game works

You will be randomly paired with another participant. Each of you will take turns playing {{ C.NUM_ROUNDS }} rounds of a game, either taking or passing. There are two different payoff sizes, the large pile and the small pile.

You will be assigned either the red or blue player each round. The red player chooses first each round.

If you take: the game ends and each player receives the listed payoffs for the turn.

If you pass: the decision is passed to the other player and the game continues. Each time the game is passed, the payoffs increase.

Payoff piles alternate each turn. This means that when it is your turn, the payoff you receive will always be the large pile while your partner receives the small pile. If it is your partners turn, then you will receive the smaller pile while they receive the large pile.
