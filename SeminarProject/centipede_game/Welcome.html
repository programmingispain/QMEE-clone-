How the game works:

You will be randomly paired with another participant. Each of you will take turns playing {{ C.NUM_ROUNDS }} rounds of a game, either taking or passing. There are several variations of the game you will be playing in nonconsecutive orders.

You will be assigned either the Red or Blue player each round. The red player chooses first each round.

If you take: the game ends and each player receives the listed payoffs for the turn.

If you pass: the decision is passed to the other player and the game continues. Each time the game is passed, the payoffs increase.

The Red Player receives the payoffs listed in red typeface, the Blue Player receives the ones in Blue.

Your total payoff will be the total of all rounds combined.

We will be monitoring while you play. If you have any questions or need help, ask an examiner.
